## Email Timing Best Practices

**Response times**:
- Initial inquiry: Within 2 hours (same day)
- Quote confirmation: Within 4 hours
- Delivery: 24 hours from payment (or sooner)
- Revision requests: Within 4 hours acknowledgment
- Follow-ups: Don't be pushy

**Follow-up schedule**:
- Day 0: Delivery
- Day 2: "How's it working?"
- Day 7: "One week check-in"
- Day 30: Product updates/future plans

**Email best practices**:
- Keep subject lines clear and actionable
- Use formatting (bold, bullets) for scannability
- Always include next steps
- Be friendly but professional
- Respond fast (builds trust)
- Personalize (use their name, reference their skill)
